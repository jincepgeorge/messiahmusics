Our History

Messiah Musics is a Christian devotional band founded by Dr Jojo P George at kottarakkara in 2000 with God’s vision. He is living in Kottarakkara with wife Raji Jojo, assistant Professor at Marhoma College Ayur and son Johan Joseph George, a high school student. Dr Jojo has completed his Bachelor of Theology from Faith Theological Seminary affiliated with Serampore University. He has performed across India with the team , UAE and United States of America. .
